JRC

Over 20 Years of Food Manufacturing Excellence

Built From Scratch in Los Angeles

JRC Culinary Group was founded with one clear vision: to bring authentic, restaurant-quality Asian dumplings to the mainstream frozen food market. We believe the best dumplings shouldn't be limited to dim sum restaurants — they should be accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Operating out of our facility in Monterey Park, we manufacture premium dumplings and frozen Asian foods using authentic recipes rooted in tradition. Every product we make reflects our commitment to real ingredients, real technique, and real flavor.

We're building toward something big — a nationally recognized brand that stands alongside the best in Asian frozen food.

Our Story

根源与传承

JRC Culinary Group began with recipes passed down through our family for generations. Growing up around Cantonese dim sum, dumplings were always part of our lives — from Lunar New Year gatherings at home to long days spent watching our mother cook in restaurant kitchens.

In 2023, JRC was founded in the San Gabriel Valley to bring those same authentic recipes to professional kitchens. Located in Monterey Park, one of the most vibrant centers of Chinese food culture in America, we produce traditional dumplings with the consistency and scale that restaurants need.

Every dumpling reflects the same care, precision, and handmade quality that our family practiced for generations.
