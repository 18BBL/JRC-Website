Our Story

About
JRC

Over 20 Years of Food Manufacturing Excellence

Built From Scratch in Los Angeles

JRC Culinary Group was founded with one clear vision: to bring authentic, restaurant-quality Asian dumplings to the mainstream frozen food market. We believe the best dumplings shouldn't be limited to dim sum restaurants — they should be accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Operating out of our facility in Monterey Park, we manufacture premium dumplings and frozen Asian foods using authentic recipes rooted in tradition. Every product we make reflects our commitment to real ingredients, real technique, and real flavor.

We're building toward something big — a nationally recognized brand that stands alongside the best in Asian frozen food. Think Bibigo. Think scale. Think JRC.
